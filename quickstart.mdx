## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
You need a valid email address and internet connection. No credit card required for the free tier. Sign up takes under 2 minutes.
</Callout>

Dezan makes it easy to organize your project documentation. Follow these steps to create your first workspace and document.

## Step 1: Sign Up for an Account

Visit [https://dezan.com.br](https://dezan.com.br) and click "Sign Up".

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Enter your email and create a password. Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="edit-3">
    Add your name and optional company details. Choose the free plan to get started immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Create Your First Workspace

Once logged in, you'll land on the dashboard at `https://dashboard.dezan.com`.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Workspace" icon="plus">
    Click the "New Workspace" button in the top right. Name it something like "My Project Docs".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Basics" icon="settings">
    Select your preferred language and enable version history. Click "Create".
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Add and Edit Your First Document

Workspaces contain documents written in MDX for rich formatting.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Document" icon="file-plus">
    Inside your workspace, click "New Document". Title it "Welcome to My Project".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Content" icon="edit">
    Use the visual editor or switch to Markdown mode. Add headings, lists, and code blocks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Publish" icon="eye">
    Preview changes live, then click "Publish" to make it live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Here's a sample Markdown snippet you can paste into your document:

```markdown
# Welcome

This is your first Dezan document.

## Features

- Rich MDX support
- Real-time collaboration
- Custom domains
```
