---
title: Cannot Invite User
description: Troubleshoot invite problems in Dezan.
---

## Issue

- The user does not receive invite emails.
- Error: "Invitation failed."

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Email Address">
    Make sure the email is correct—no typos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask User to Check Spam">
    Sometimes invites end up in the spam folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend Invitation">
    Go to Project > Members > Resend the invite.
  </Step>
</Steps>
