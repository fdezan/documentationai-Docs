---
title: Organize Your Documentation
description: Simple guide to structuring docs in Dezan.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Folders">
    Click **Add Folder** in your project to group docs by topic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Documentation Pages">
    Use **New Page** inside a folder to create and edit docs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rearrange Order">
    Drag and drop pages or folders to change their order as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
