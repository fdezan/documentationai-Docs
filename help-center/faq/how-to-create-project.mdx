---
title: How do I create a new project?
description: Quick steps to add a project in Dezan.
---

## Steps

1. Log in to your Dezan dashboard.
2. Click **New Project** in the sidebar.
3. Enter a project name and description.
4. Click **Create**.
5. Your project appears in your list.

## Info

You can edit details later from the project settings. Only workspace admins can create new projects.
