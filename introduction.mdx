## Overview

Dezan provides a powerful documentation platform that helps you organize, manage, and share project documentation effortlessly. You can create structured spaces for your teams, collaborate in real-time, and keep everything searchable and up-to-date. Whether managing software projects, internal wikis, or client deliverables, Dezan simplifies your workflow and ensures knowledge stays accessible.

## Quick Start

Get up and running with Dezan in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user">
    Sign up at `https://dezan.com` with your email. Verify your account to access the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Space" icon="folder">
    Click "New Space" and name it for your project, like "My Project Docs". Add a description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add First Document" icon="plus">
````markdown
# Welcome to My Project

This is your first document.
````

    Use the editor to create markdown pages with embeds and components.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Collaborators" icon="users">
    Share the space link or add emails directly. Set roles like editor or viewer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
